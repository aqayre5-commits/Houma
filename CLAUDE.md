# CLAUDE.md

## Operating Sequence
1. Evaluate concept against kill criteria.
2. If failed, stop and output failure report only.
3. If passed, define v1 scope and route map.
4. Produce design system and page inventory.
5. Produce component-state map and responsive rules.
6. Produce engineering pack.
7. Produce monetization and analytics pack.
8. Produce QA and release pack.
9. Produce final implementation-ready file tree.
10. Produce final ZIP-ready handoff structure.

## Hard Rules
- Output ranked lists, tables, and build-ready specs only in docs.
- Do not add non-v1 subsystems.
- Do not add auth, billing, or booking in v1.
- Keep city-service flow under 4 clicks.
- Maintain visible non-official disclaimer on critical pages.
- Every market claim in docs must be labeled Verified Fact, Inference, or Recommendation.
