# product_definition.md

## Product Definition
| Field | Definition |
|---|---|
| Product name | Houma |
| Core promise | Find the right local office and required preparation in fewer than 4 clicks |
| Audience | Morocco residents in Casablanca, Rabat, Tanger; families; first-time applicants; time-constrained users |
| Primary problem | Users know the service they need but not the correct neighborhood office, exact prep list, or whether an online step exists before the visit |
| Primary value | Decision-ready local guidance with trust signals, source links, outbound map links, and error-report fallback |
| Non-official status | Mandatory on every critical template |
| v1 geography | Casablanca, Rabat, Tanger |
| v1 service families | Identity, civil status, attestations |
| v1 monetization | AdSense + clearly labeled local sponsor blocks + commercial CTA modules |
| v1 excluded systems | Auth, user accounts, billing, appointments, live chat, multilingual CMS |

## Primary User Jobs
| Rank | Job To Be Done | Success Condition |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Know where to go | Named office or office type is visible above the fold |
| 2 | Know what to prepare | Required documents, fees status, and offline/online path are visible without scroll traps |
| 3 | Know if the trip is worth it | Verification date, source list, and fallback note are visible |
| 4 | Get directions fast | Open map in one tap |
| 5 | Choose an alternative | Nearby office or online option appears if primary office is unavailable |

## Non-Goals
| Rank | Exclusion | Reason |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Appointment booking | High ops and legal risk |
| 2 | Identity/authenticated user accounts | No day-one utility gain |
| 3 | Payments or paywall | Trust and scope drag |
| 4 | Full national city coverage | Thin-page and ops risk |
| 5 | Real-time office hours guarantee | Source volatility |

## Trust and Editorial Rules
| Rule | Requirement |
|---|---|
| Disclaimer | “Private guide. Not an official government website.” visible site-wide |
| Source block | Mandatory on city-service and office pages |
| Verification date | Mandatory on city-service and office pages |
| Error correction | “Report an issue” CTA mandatory on critical pages |
| Thin-page control | No indexable page without 4 unique content modules |
